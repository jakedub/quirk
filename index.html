About Me

I am originally from the suburbs of Indianapolis. I love trying new things, home brewing, rooting for the Packers and the Colts, reading, baking, barbequing, and most importantly education. I have a Bachelor's of Arts in Political Science from the University of Indianapolis, Master's of Public Affairs with a concentration in Nonprofit Management, and attended The Iron Yard to become a Full Stack Engineer.


I firmly believe the best thing a person can do is to never stop learning. If you want to work with a developer that has a deep passion for learning, enjoys working with others, driven to be the best Ruby on Rails developer, and lives to share knowledge with others then I'm your guy. Please take a look at my skill sets, projects or feel free to contact me.
